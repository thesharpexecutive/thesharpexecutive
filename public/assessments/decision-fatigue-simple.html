Take Control of Your Decision-Making Power

As an executive, you make countless decisions daily. This assessment will help you understand how decision fatigue might be affecting your performance and provide insights for optimization.

1. How do you typically feel when making important decisions late in the day?
2. How often do you find yourself avoiding or postponing decisions?
3. When faced with multiple options, how do you typically respond?
4. How has your decision-making quality changed throughout your career?
5. How do you feel after making a series of important decisions?
6. How often do you second-guess decisions you've made?
7. What's your approach to minor daily decisions (like what to wear or eat)?
8. How do you handle conflicting advice from team members or advisors?
